Preserves Cash Flow

One of the last things a business wants is to be low on cash. This can severely restrict operations and their future investments. And for individuals, you need cash on hand for emergencies, investment opportunities, and other life events. With used heavy equipment financing, you'll be able to preserve your cash flow.

Makes Budgeting Easier

When you finance used heavy equipment, you'll know your exact monthly payment every month. This helps for budgeting, especially if you were relying on heavy equipment rentals instead with costs that varied from month-to-month.

Tax Write-Off

When you purchase heavy equipment, you can usually write off the interest paid if you are a business. You also can depreciate your heavy equipment. Speak to your accountant who can explain the advantages with regards to tax law.

Use Your Equipment To Make the Payments

When you own heavy equipment as a business, you can use the money you make doing work for others to make the payment for you, which is similar to how rent works. You get the best of both worlds when you use your heavy equipment and it pays for itself.
